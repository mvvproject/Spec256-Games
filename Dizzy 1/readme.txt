Game: Dizzy I
Publisher: Codemasters
Author(s): Oliver twins

The most famous egg in 8-bit history gets a facelift in this 256 colour version of the original Dizzy game. Help Dizzy find the cure to athelete's foot - something that is troubling his fair land. And in the process destroy the Evil Wizard Zaks. No,I'm not making this up!
